# Contradiction Detection

The Contradictions API allows you to detect and manage contradictions in your knowledge base. This helps maintain data quality and consistency across your documents and information.

## Overview

The Contradictions API provides:

- **Contradiction Detection**: Automatically identify contradictions in your data
- **Quality Management**: Maintain data quality and consistency
- **Conflict Resolution**: Help resolve conflicting information
- **Data Validation**: Validate information across your knowledge base

## Endpoints

### Contradiction Management

- `GET /contradictions` - List contradictions
